About Me

Born and raised in Rochester, NY, I started my love of technology when I took apart and fixed small electronics and built solar chargers. After two years at Rochester Institute of Technology studying electrical and software engineering I changed directions and schools, finishing with a B.S. in Leadership, Ethics and Organizational Sustainability (LEO) at Saint Joseph's University. After several analyst roles, I've found my passion with data science, and will be receiving my M.S. in Data Science in June 2021.
